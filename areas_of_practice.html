CONTRACT AND LEASE REVIEW

Many businesses and individuals make the critical mistake of signing documents without reading them thoroughly. Often those that do carefully read documents prior to execution, do not understand all the legal ramifications arising out of the language contained in a contract or other binding document. The very best use of a lawyer is before a legal problem arises. Attorney fees for consultation and advise prior to contract execution is exponentially smaller than fees associated with resolving litigation arising out of a breach of contract claim. Our firm can provide opinion letters and sage advise regarding the ramifications of the language contained in any contract whether it is a lease, insurance policy and other contract.

CONTRACT AND LEASE DRAFTING

The corollary of contract review if contract drafting. Often the party who drafts the contract is the one receiving the benefit of better terms and conditions. Our firm can prepare contracts for business acquisitions, property purchases and leases arrangements.
