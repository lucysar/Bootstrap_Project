About Ronald B. Fadel

Ronald B. Fadel has practiced law for twenty years. He graduated from the University of Louisville School of Law in 1996 with a joint MBA/JD degree. During law school Mr. Fadel served as a member of the University of Louisville’s law review. Since graduation and passing the Ohio and Kentucky bars, he has represented large corporate clients as well as victimized individuals in a variety of legal situations. As a business and property owner himself, Mr. Fadel has an emphatic viewpoint of the challenges that entrepreneurs, small business owners and landlords face. His personal experience starting businesses growing them to acquisition targets has provided Mr. Fadel with the experience necessary to help any business client navigate the most complex legal issues to a beneficial endpoint.
